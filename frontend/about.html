About Jackal Tech Ltd

Driving Technological Innovation to Solve Global Challenges

About Us

At Jackal Tech, our mission is to drive technological innovation that addresses global challenges.

We are dedicated to creating impactful innovations that shape the future.

Founded in late 2019 and rebranded in 2021 in Kigali, Rwanda, Jackal Tech has grown into a leader in technology solutions, with a focus on healthcare, education, finance, manufacturing, agriculture, and retail sectors.

Our dedicated team of experts works tirelessly to deliver cutting-edge solutions that empower businesses and communities to thrive in the digital age.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in technological innovation, driving sustainable growth and improving lives worldwide.

Our Mission

  • To develop and implement innovative solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients.
  • To foster a culture of continuous improvement and excellence.
  • To promote sustainable practices and social responsibility in all our endeavors.

Our Values

Integrity

We adhere to the highest standards of ethical conduct in all our actions.

Innovation

We embrace change and encourage creativity to deliver top-notch solutions.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, ensuring high quality and continuous improvement.

Customer Focus

We prioritize our customers' needs and work to exceed their expectations.

Social Responsibility

We are committed to making a positive impact on society and the environment.

Our Story

Jackal Tech Ltd. began with a vision to create a tech-savvy and solution-oriented community. Since our founding in late 2019 in Kigali, Rwanda, we have grown into a company known for its innovative solutions and impactful projects.

Our mission is to drive technological innovation that addresses global challenges. We empower the next generation through education and skill-building, fostering a community dedicated to finding practical solutions to critical global issues.

At Jackal Tech Ltd., our vision is to be a leader in technology, consistently creating impactful innovations that elevate scientific understanding, transform societies, and improve lives worldwide.
