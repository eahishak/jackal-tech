About Us

At Jackal Tech, our mission is to drive technological innovation that addresses global challenges. We are dedicated to creating solutions that not only meet the immediate needs of our clients but also have a lasting positive impact on the world. Our approach involves leveraging cutting-edge technologies to develop innovative products and services that solve complex problems in various sectors, including healthcare, education, finance, manufacturing, agriculture, and retail

We are dedicated to creating impactful innovations that shape the future.

Founded in late 2019 and rebranded in 2021 in Kigali, Rwanda, Jackal Tech has grown into a leader in technology solutions, with a focus on healthcare, education, finance, manufacturing, agriculture, and retail sectors.

Our dedicated team of experts works tirelessly to deliver cutting-edge solutions that empower businesses and communities to thrive in the digital age.
