Jackal Tech Ltd Quarterly Update (July-September)

July - September 2024

Dear Jackal Tech Family,

It has been an exciting quarter at Jackal Tech Ltd! We’ve made significant strides in our programs, partnerships, and growth, impacting the tech community globally. Here’s a recap of what we’ve achieved from July to September 2024.

Jackal Tech Programs

1. Internship Program

We welcomed interns to our Summer 2024 Software Engineering Internship Program, selecting the top 64 from a global pool of applicants. These interns come from 14 different countries, showcasing our global reach and dedication to diversity:

  • Rwanda: 26%
  • Pakistan: 19%
  • Kenya: 16%
  • Vietnam: 11%
  • United States: 6%
  • South Africa: 5%
  • Tanzania: 5%

Interns from multiple countries are joining us to innovate and learn.

2. Fellowship Program

Congratulations to the 231 fellows admitted into the Jackal Tech Fellowship Program 2024! Fellows are joining departments such as Software Engineering, IT, Business, Marketing, Data Science, and Project Management.

Fellows Spotlight: Nice Arlene Rugolirwera (Marketing) [left-most], Wiseman Kamanga (SWE) [second from left], Ida Idèle Nkouadjo (Marketing) [third from left], Andy Kelly Irimbere (SWE) [right-most]

Jackal Tech fellows are taking innovation to the next level.

3. Apprenticeship Program

We welcomed 19 apprentices this year, representing 3% of the total applicants in fields such as Software Engineering, Data Science, and Project Management.

Apprentice Spotlight: SHEMA TUYIZERE Patrick (SWE) [left], Enock Mutabazi (Project Management) [right]

Our apprentices are learning and contributing to cutting-edge projects.

4. Externship Programs

Our externship programs continue to provide students and professionals with real-world projects across industries. Stay tuned for more updates!

Jackal Tech Events, Workshops & Competitions

In September, I had the incredible opportunity to speak at the KPDC Interactive Talk, hosted by Laurien Ikuzwe, Managing Director of KELP Ltd. It was a moment to reflect on my journey, standing alongside some of the brightest young entrepreneurs of our time.

KPDC Interactive Talk with Laurien Ikuzwe, Managing Director at Kelp Ltd.

We also introduced the World Robot Contest 2024 Teachers Training, empowering 379 individuals to nurture future innovators in collaboration with Afribot Robotics from Kenya, ZMRobo, and Dassault Systèmes.

World Robot Contest Teachers Training 2024.

Membership Partner: Rwanda ICT Chamber

At Jackal Tech Ltd, we are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology to create cutting-edge solutions that drive digital transformation. As a proud member of the Rwanda ICT Chamber, we are committed to influencing the technological landscape with our innovative products and services.

Rwanda ICT Chamber - Driving technology in Africa.

Partner: Afribot Robotics, Kenya

We partnered with Afribot Robotics to develop cutting-edge robotic solutions for the future. Our collaboration aims to empower innovation and drive technological advancements across Africa.

Afribot Robotics Partnership.

Our Smart Solutions

We’re committed to implementing smart solutions for urban development, environmental monitoring, and more. These solutions are designed to enhance quality of life and promote sustainable development in Rwanda and beyond.

Ian Mukasa, Strategic Partnerships Director

Smart solutions for sustainable urban development.

Our team continues to grow as we onboard new staff members who are key to Jackal Tech’s success in delivering innovative technology solutions.

Our new team members are driving innovation forward.
