    Company Overview 🏢

    Jackal Tech Ltd. is a pioneering technology company based in Kigali, Rwanda. Since our inception in January 2020, we have been committed to driving technological innovation that addresses global challenges.

    Mission 🌟 & Vision 👁️

    Mission: To drive technological innovation that addresses global challenges. *Together to make it a reality.*

    Vision: To be a leader in technology, creating impactful innovations that elevate scientific understanding, transform societies, and improve lives worldwide. *Together to make it a reality.*
